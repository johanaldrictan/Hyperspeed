1. Player can move left to right using Physics based movement(Rigidbody2D)
2. Player can jump using Physics based movement(Rigidbody2D)... player can also choose to
   fall faster
3. Player collides with platforms and obstacles
4. When player goes out of screen, player triggers loss state. 
   When player collides with coin, player collects coin and coin is destroyed
5. Platforms, Coins, and Obstacles are randomly generated as the game progresses

Extra Credit: The goal of the game is to survive and collect coins until you reach the 
score threshold. The level will scroll to the left and move faster the closer you reach
the score threshold.
